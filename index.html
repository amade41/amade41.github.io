Meet My Cats

The real stars of the show

Dora is a sweet and affection cat that will let you pet her all day long. She loves napping in her cat tower and sleeping in funny positions. She also loves carrying around fuzzy cat toys and meowing very loudly for some reason (maybe to signal she's caught the prey and it is time to celebrate). She also loves trying to open cabinet doors and sneaking inside them to hide. She likes cuddling with you at night even if that includes smothering your face with her entire body and purring very loudly.
Will is an agent of chaos but is also very affection when he's not running around terrorizing everyone in the household and begging for treats. He loves playing fetch and will even bring the toy back to you about 50% of the time (the other 50% of the time he will give you a look that asks why aren't you throwing my toy that is half way across the house). He loves pretending like he's Isaac Newton discovering gravity as he's fond of knocking small objects from great heights. He also loves putting his toys in his water bowl and then dropping the wet toy on your head in the middle of the night. He's terrified of slippers/shoes and treats them as mysterious evil creatures that it's best to hide from. No one has stepped on his tail with a shoe so I am not sure the origin of this fear.
